Welcome to Husky Park!

Here are some tips and guidelines for enjoying your park visit! You can also tap on the buttons below to find a map of the park, and detailed instructions for all activities.

Rules

  • No gloves when petting

    All our huskies are friendly, but some are expert glove thieves, so keep any gloves and handwarmers safe in your pocket or purse, when patting any of our huskies.

  • Some signs say "No petting"

    Some of our husky enclosures have a sign, that says NO PETTING. These dogs are not aggressive, but they can get excited very easily so to avoid any accidents, do not pet these dogs.
    If you have kids with you, it's best to always ask one of the guides at the park, to make sure which dogs are best for small children to interact with.
