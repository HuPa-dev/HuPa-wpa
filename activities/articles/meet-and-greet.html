Meet & Greet tips!


  • No gloves for petting or feeding

    Huskies curious, and very interested in gloves. They may grab them, and eating gloves is bad for them. Keep your gloves and handwarmers safe in your pockets or bag.

    During the winter season, the Meet & Greet area has fireplaces for warming up your hands.

    Running or chasing makes huskies more excited and can confuse younger dogs. Stay calm, relaxed, and let the huskies come to you. Help us train good habits into the puppies ğŸ¾
